Tables for Your Schema

Products Table
- product_id (Primary Key)
- name
- description
- category_id (Foreign Key)
- price
- stock_quantity
- supplier_id (Foreign Key)
- created_at
- updated_at


Categories Table
- category_id (Primary Key)
- category_name
- description


Sales Table:
- sale_id → Unique identifier for each sale.
- customer_id → Links to the customer who made the purchase.
- total_price → Total amount for the entire sale.
- sale_date → Date and time when the sale occurred.


Sales_Items Table:
- sale_item_id → Unique identifier for each sale item.
- sale_id → Links to the sale in the Sales Table.
- product_id → Links to the product being sold.
- quantity_sold → How many units of the product were purchased.
- unit_price → Price of the product at the time of sale.


Customers Table (If tracking customer purchases)
- customer_id (Primary Key)
- name
- email
- phone_number
- address

Employees Table (If tracking staff managing inventory)
- employee_id (Primary Key)
- name
- role
- contact_info

Relationships & Keys
- product_id in the Products Table is linked to product_id in the Sales Table.
- category_id in the Products Table is linked to category_id in the Categories Table.
- supplier_id in the Products Table is linked to supplier_id in the Suppliers Table.
- customer_id in the Sales Table is linked to customer_id in the Customers Table.
